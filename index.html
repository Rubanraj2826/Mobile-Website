FAQ

On Oppo devices, expandable and collapsible sections allow users to manage information more efficiently by hiding or showing extra content. For example, in the settings or apps like contacts or messages, you might see categories or submenus that can be tapped to expand or collapse, helping to keep the interface organized and clean.

In the settings menu on Oppo phones running ColorOS, you will find categories like Wi-Fi, Display, Bluetooth, and more. Tapping on any of these categories will reveal additional settings or options. Some settings can be collapsed again to simplify the interface and reduce clutter.

On Oppo devices, swipe down on the screen to access Quick Settings (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, brightness, etc.), where you’ll find several options that can be expanded or collapsed. In apps, if there’s hidden content or options, look for a small arrow or bar next to the section header to reveal additional settings or information.
